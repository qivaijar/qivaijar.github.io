## About

I began my career as an Artificial Intelligence (AI) Engineer in May 2020 at Prosa.ai, where I contributed to the development of high-quality Text-to-Speech (TTS) voice models specifically tailored for Bahasa Indonesia. My responsibilities included data collection, dataset management, training toolkit exploration, TTS model training and evaluation, and continuous model improvement. I also conducted research in paralinguistics, focusing on tasks such as emotion classification based on speech signals and COVID-19 diagnosis using cough recordings.

<br/>

## Responsibilities

- Developed TTS models combining transformers and GANs to generate speech from acoustic features.
- Researched TTS architectures and training toolkits to identify optimal solutions for Bahasa Indonesia.
- Explored AI model optimization frameworks (ONNX, OpenVINO, and TensorRT) to improve inference speed.
- Built AI models for paralinguistic tasks, including a COVID-19 diagnosis system based on cough recordings.
- Developed an emotion classification system based on speech signals.
